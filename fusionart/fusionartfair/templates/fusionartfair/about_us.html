Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between artists and their audience, we recognise a need for a platform where artists can showcase their incredible talents and also provide a space for meaningful connections and conversation with art collectors and enthusiasts.

Meet the Founder of Fusion Art Fair

Joanne Chon

Step into the Fusion Art Fair and meet the visionary behind the scenes

Driven by a deep-seated passion for art and a desire to provide a platform for artists to showcase their talent, Joanne decided to take matters into her own hands and launch Fusion Art Fair. Her aim was clear: to curate an event that not only showcases the diverse talents of artist but also facilitates connection between artists and potential buyers.

Joanne brings a wealth of experience to the table. Having served as a fair director for a reputable art fair based in London, she possesses a deep understanding of the intricate dynamics of the art world. Her tenure in this role has afforded her invaluable insights into the inner workings of the art world and developed an understanding of the support and aspirations of both artists and art buyers.

In addition to her role in art fair direction, Joanne also boasts a rich background in the exhibition world, where she has honed her skills in event planning and execution.
